My story

Made by someone who really cares.

My name is Katya. I love people and their stories. I’m a trained raw-cake crafter and I’m training as a coach because I care about both how we treat our bodies and how we treat our minds — they’re connected.

My first impulse was to learn to make raw cakes for my own and my family’s health. Then I thought of everyone who can’t eat the usual cakes — allergies, sugar, dairy — and wanted them to have a beautiful, healthier choice too. That’s when I realised I wanted to change how cake is seen. For most of us, “cake” means sugar, guilt, something to regret later. I truly believe it can mean the opposite: real nourishment that happens to taste incredible.

Raw Love Cakes are built from good ingredients — and good ingredients are the only way to a good result. That’s what goes into every box: healthy ingredients, care for the person who’ll open it, and no waste behind it — in raw-cake making it’s easy to use every last ingredient, so it’s care twice over: for you and for the planet.

Good to know

Questions, answered.

Are the cakes really healthy?

Yes — they’re built from whole, real ingredients: home-made plant milks, nuts, fruit, berries, cacao and coconut butter. No refined sugar, no flour, no dairy, no eggs. Naturally vegan and gluten-free.

Do you cater to nut allergies?

Honestly, no — nuts are a core ingredient in almost everything I make, so if you have a serious nut allergy these cakes aren’t safe for you, and I’d never want to risk it. (Final wording to confirm with Katya. [P5])

How much notice do you need?

Usually 1–3 days. Some recipes ferment for 24 hours; a few can be ready the next day. For events, the more notice the better.

How do I pay and collect?

We sort the details when you get in touch — pickup or delivery, and payment by invoice/transfer. (To confirm. [P6])

Can I order for a company or event?

Absolutely — see the events section. Finger-food minis, platters, and larger orders from ~50 pieces.
