Is it feasible?

Yes — and here's the honest picture.

The slice geometry, the click-to-open, the lift-out animation — all standard web (SVG + a little JavaScript), no special tools or cost. What you're looking at on the left is the real thing, not a mockup. A few things to decide before it goes on the live site:

✅ Easy / done here

  • 8 clickable slices, one per flavour
  • Each a different colour
  • Lift-out on click
  • Ingredients open beside it
  • Works on phone (tap) and desktop

🎨 Choices to make

  • Open beside the cake (shown) or scroll down to the list — both are easy; I can show the scroll version too
  • Real cake-photo slices (shown) or simpler flat colours
  • Labels on slices, or reveal-on-tap only (shown)

🧩 To get right

  • Accessibility — keyboard + screen-reader friendly (foundations already in)
  • What a first-time visitor sees before tapping
  • How it sits next to the existing card menu (replace it, or both?)

My recommendation: keep it as a playful hero for the Cakes section, with the familiar card list still underneath for people who just want to scan. Best of both — delight and clarity.
